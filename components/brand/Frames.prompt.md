More 国风 frames (set `window.LF_ICON_BASE`):

```jsx
<OrnateBar value={64} tone="teal" label="推演中…" />     {/* 血条/进度条 */}
<Banner variant="teal" size={64}>Lv5</Banner>            {/* 旗幡 crest banner */}
<FeaturePanel style={{width:460}}>                       {/* 卷轴/匾额 scene dialog */}
  <h2>万象模板，一键起步</h2>
</FeaturePanel>
```

`OrnateBar` tones: teal | gold | moon | cinnabar. `Banner` variants: teal | paper. `FeaturePanel` content overlays the misty interior below the pagoda roof.
