Icon-only square button for toolbars (放大、重新生成、导出 etc).

```jsx
<IconButton label="放大预览"><i data-lucide="maximize-2" /></IconButton>
<IconButton variant="surface" active><i data-lucide="grid-2x2" /></IconButton>
```

`variant`: ghost | surface | gold. `size`: sm | md | lg. `active` for toggled state.
